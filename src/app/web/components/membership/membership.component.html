Join CreditQ as member and stay ahead of others. By joining CreditQ as a member you not only save a lot of money but you also cut down your debts and other hassles. Its like you make completely fair business dealings as you do not deal with defaulters.

Business these days is scary for some. The bad people in business have made it a nightmare specially for MSMEs who are largely less informed and being new in the business, end up in the trap of defaulters.

What are the benefits of becoming a CreditQ member?
CreditQ gives an insight into the reputation of the client

Becoming a CreditQ member has a lot of advantages. First, it provides you complete information on the prospective client and helps you identify if the client has defaulted on payments before and will it be feasible or safe to do a business dealing with the said client.

Scope for settlement of payment

Next, once the defaulter name is added on the list of defaulters on CreditQ, the defaulter client might approach you and offer a settlement. You have the right to settle the payment dispute and ask for removal of the name of the client from the defaulters list.

Do business in the market fearlessly

CreditQ helps you take informed decision about business dealing with new business or clients and thus helps protect you from any danger of default in payment or other hassles. It saves you from a lot of trouble.

How I can join CreditQ?

If you are MSME and has a valid GST number then you can fill the form on the CreditQ portal or the CreditQ app and join as a member by paying the membership fee. You need to enter your contact information, GST number, email address, company address and other information at the time of registration on CreditQ.

How much is the membership fee?

Joining CreditQ is easy, quick and it does not dig a hole in your pocket. By paying a minimal fee of Rs1000/- any Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) which has a valid GST registration number can join. Once registered the members can add defaulters, and Credit Report of other clients or businesses and check whether the client or business they are about to make a deal with is on the list of defaulter or not.
