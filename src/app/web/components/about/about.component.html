CreditQ – Aapke Vyapaar Ka Chaukidaar

Let us take the example of SMB owner Mr. Kumar in order to understand what wonders CreditQ can bring to your business dealings

Small business owner Mr. Kumar’s food packaging startup supplies packaging material. Lately, Mr. Kumar was hired by a client which gave him a huge order. As promised Mr. Kumar supplied material on time and produced an invoice for payment. At first, the client delayed payment. Now, even after several reminders and finally even after a civil suit, Mr. Kumar still awaits payment. As the payment amount was stuck, not only Mr. Kumar’s is under huge debt, he has also lost his peace of mind in business.

So, what exactly went wrong in this deal? Was Mr. Kumar at fault anywhere?

Is there anything Mr. Kumar must have done beforehand in order to avoid this situation?

Certainly not, Mr. Kumar did what was part of the deal, but it was due to the neglect by the client that his payment was stuck. However, Mr. Kumar was at indeed at fault as he did not survey or research well beforehand.

This is where CreditQ – The Vyapaar Ka Chaukidaar, comes into picture

If you are making a business deal CreditQ gives you the complete picture about the client, its credit history and information about whether the client is a defaulter in payments in its dealings with other vendors.

Its like giving businessmen a complete overview of their new client and helping them in making an informed decision about business dealing with the new client.

If your prospective client is there in CreditQ’s database as a defaulter, beware of it, don’t deal with the client, and you might like to avoid facing what Mr. Kumar had to.

CreditQ is like your virtual business assistant Aapka saathi hamesha

It informs, educates and helps you take all the informed business decisions on whether to deal with your prospective client or not. CreditQ is a platform for MSMEs where they can check other businessmen and verify if they are cheater or not before doing any business with them. Journey of our company

Founded this year on May 20, 2019, CreditQ is the brainchild of Mukta Mittal who spearheads the Private Limited company with headquarters in Jaipur, Rajasthan.

CreditQ’s Inception

Initially it was an idea that used to make Mukta constantly strive to come up with a solution to the woes of MSMEs who fall prey to habitual defaulters in the business world.

Concept to Creation

As Mukta hails from a business class family, she has seen the ups and down of business and closely observed how things turn weird for businessmen if clients fail to make the payment on time. As small business owners do not have large working capital they have to entirely rely on regular flow and if in unforeseen circumstances, capital is stuck then they end up in huge debt.

In order to address this situation and protect MSMEs from such financial crisis, Mukta came up with the concept of CreditQ, a platform which informs, warns and protects them from defaulters.

CreditQ’s Mission Vision

CreditQ’s mission is to be the eyes and ear of all MSMEs and help them identify defaulters before they start doing business with them.

The way ahead… Milestones to achieve

Being the first Credit Information Reporting Company in India, CreditQ has set its goal to be the Vyapaar ka Chaukidaar for all MSMEs. It also sets its target of becoming the platform which business owners consult before making a deal with a businesses or clients.

In the 1st month of its launch – CreditQ will target about 2,000 GST customers Jaipur.

In the 3rd month – CreditQ will target about 17,000 GST holder in seven big divisions of Rajasthan viz. Kota, Ajmer, Jodhpur, Bikaner, Udaipur, Jaipur, Bharatpur/Alwar.

During the sixth month of its launch – CreditQ will target about 1,17,000 GST holders in half of North India (UP, Delhi, Haryana, Punjab, Chandigarh, and others)

By the end of year and before its first anniversary – CreditQ will target about 3,00,000 GST holders in North West India

In two years, CreditQ hopes to cover around 10,00,000 GST users in rest of India.

Later, CreditQ will add all the GST users on this platform.
