Who we are?

CreditQ is a web & app-based startup that deals in a revolutionary financial mobile & web application for B2B MSMEs. Its a B2B app for Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) that help them in solving the credit problem they largely face and to make them aware of the fraudulent and cheaters in the society. We offer solutions to the many B2B MSMEs in identifying frauds and double-dealers by providing credit scores of an organization, company, firm, proprietor or business.

Membership

Join CreditQ as member and stay ahead of others. By joining CreditQ as a member you not only save a lot of money but you also cut down your debts and other hassles. Its like you make completely fair business dealings as you do not deal with defaulters.

Add Defaulter

Adding a defaulter is very easy and completely free for CreditQ registered members. By adding a defaulter, MSMEs not only help themselves but also make sure that other MSMEs do not fall prey to the defaulter. The Defaulters will be exposed all over India as the Credit Information Report and defaulters list will be available to all the members across India.

Credit Information Report

CreditQis a platform where MSMEs and businesses enroll themselves and seek information about other businesses and clients before entering into a business deal. Being a dependable source of authentic information about companies and clients, business owners trust CreditQ and seek its help to make decisions before making any business deal with an altogether new business or client.

Settle Payment

CreditQ lists defaulters identified and added by the registered members. However, if the concerned parties, the registered member and the defaulter agrees and settles the payment then the registered member could remove defaulter’s name from the list of defaulters on CreditQ.
